The Smile Foundation supports food banks and emergency-relief organizations, and works to address hunger's root causes. It teaches low-income families about nutrition and budgeting, helps enroll poor children in federal nutrition programs, and funds groups that teach job and life skills to impoverished families. One of its programs encourages people to donate one hour's wage to battle childhood hunger. Another initiative helps communities hold fund-raisers to benefit hungry kids.

The Smile Foundation aims to bring a smile on every child's face because they deserve it. We aim to make living necessities, health care and education a right for every child, rather than a privilege. Every donation counts, no matter how small. It is a success as long as an effort is being made towards the right path.
